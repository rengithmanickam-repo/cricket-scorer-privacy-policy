Privacy Policy for CricScorer

Last Updated: February 6, 2025

Introduction

CricScorer ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

CricScorer does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information or data.

All data created within the app, including:

  • Team information
  • Player information
  • Match data
  • Statistics
  • Scores and records

is stored locally on your device only. We do not have access to this data, and it is never transmitted to our servers or any third parties.

Data Storage

All app data is stored locally on your iOS device using local storage mechanisms. This data remains on your device and is not backed up to cloud services unless you explicitly enable iCloud backup for your device.

Third-Party Services

CricScorer does not integrate with any third-party analytics services, advertising networks, or data collection services.

Children's Privacy

CricScorer is suitable for users of all ages. Since we do not collect any personal information, we do not knowingly collect information from children under 13 years of age.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.
